Who We Are

We are a group of security engineers and penetration testers, securing the world one implementation and test at a time.

We are a security-focused team specializing in penetration testing and technical audits across cloud, network, and application environments. Our work spans mobile and web application assessments, including in-depth API and source code reviews. Beyond client work, we build scripts and tools to automate tasks for fun, explore new vulnerabilities, AI integrations, and share with the community. Our mission is to help organizations secure their systems before attackers do.

Discover

We begin by identifying critical assets, potential attack surfaces, and business logic exposures. This phase includes scoping and understanding the environment — cloud, web, mobile, APIs, and infrastructure.

Analyze

We evaluate systems for misconfigurations, outdated software, exposed services, and insecure code. Our methodology blends manual and automated techniques to uncover hidden or complex vulnerabilities.

Exploit

We simulate real-world attacks using custom scripts and tools to validate risks and demonstrate impact. This step ensures findings are not theoretical — they are actionable and relevant.

Secure

We provide prioritized remediation guidance, tailored to your environment and development workflows. Our goal is to not just find vulnerabilities — but help you close them effectively and efficiently.

What We Do

We do what we have to do to make you secure and keep you secure.

At HNLabs, we help organizations secure their software, infrastructure, and development workflows through expert-driven offensive security assessments and DevSecOps engineering. Our core services include penetration testing, secure code reviews, cloud security assessments, and CI/CD pipeline hardening. We partner closely with engineering teams to identify vulnerabilities early, automate security controls, and embed best practices across the SDLC. Whether you're launching a new product or scaling your platform, we provide the tools, expertise, and guidance to ensure your systems are resilient, secure, and built for the real world.

Application Security

We perform comprehensive assessments to identify and remediate vulnerabilities in your web, mobile, and desktop applications. Our approach includes threat modeling, secure coding guidance, and both manual and automated testing to ensure your applications are secure.

DevSecOps

We integrate security into your development pipelines without slowing down delivery. From automated static and dynamic analysis to secret scanning, dependency checks, and infrastructure-as-code reviews, we enable secure CI/CD integration. We also review the current pipeline for security issues.

Penetration Testing

Our team simulates real-world attacks on your applications, APIs, cloud environments, and infrastructure to uncover critical vulnerabilities before attackers do. We provide actionable reports, retesting, and remediation guidance based on industry best practices like OWASP and PTES.

Secure Design

We help teams architect systems that are secure by default. From security design reviews to architectural threat modeling, we ensure your platforms follow secure patterns, apply the principle of least privilege, and reduce attack surfaces before any code is written.

Vulnerability Management

We establish and optimize your vulnerability management program by identifying, prioritizing, and tracking security issues across your assets. Whether integrating scanners, triaging findings, or aligning with SLAs, we help you stay ahead of threats with structured remediation workflows.

API Security

We secure REST, GraphQL, and third-party APIs by identifying vulnerabilities such as broken authentication, excessive data exposure, and business logic flaws. Our testing covers both public and internal APIs, using a manual and automated techniques.hardened.

External Attack Surface Mapping

We help identify and assess your organization's exposed digital assets — including domains, subdomains, IP ranges, cloud resources, and third-party integrations. Using advanced reconnaissance and OSINT techniques, we help reduce your attack surface.

Security Training

We deliver targeted training to help teams build secure software and improve security awareness. Our sessions cover CI/CD security, secure coding, application security, and phishing simulations, tailored to your environment and delivered through interactive workshops or virtual labs.

Threat Modeling & Risk Assessment

We guide teams through structured threat modeling exercises to uncover design-level risks and prioritize mitigation strategies. By aligning with frameworks like STRIDE, we help you proactively identify potential threats before code is written or deployed..
